Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry? +

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our support line for immediate assistance.

Is AI Buddy suitable for crisis situations? +

While AI Buddy provides emotional support, it is not a replacement for emergency mental health services. If you're in crisis, please contact your local emergency services or a crisis hotline immediately.

Can I use AI Buddy without creating an account? +

Yes, you can access basic features without an account, but creating an account allows you to save your progress, track your emotional journey, and access personalized recommendations.

Is my data and privacy protected? +

Absolutely. We take your privacy seriously. All your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent.
